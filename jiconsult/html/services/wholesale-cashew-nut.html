Dive into the delicious world of cashews with our selection! Cashew Nut come from trees native to Brazil, and are now cultivated around the globe. Bursting with plenty of plant-based goodness such as fibre, heart healthy fats, protein and minerals like copper magnesium iron phosphorus selenium manganese they provide a wealth of health benefits – energy production brain function immunity strong bones plus more! Shop online for various grade types that can be purchased in broken or unbroken form when ordering wholesale quantities. Yummmmmmm!!!!!

It is eaten on its own, used in recipes, or processed into cashew cheese or cashew butter. The cashew apple is a light reddish to yellow fruit, whose pulp can be processed into a sweet, astringent fruit drink or distilled into liquor. The shell of the cashew seed yields derivatives that can be used in many applications from lubricants to paints. Originally native to north-eastern Brazil, the tree is now widely cultivated in Vietnam, Nigeria and India as major production countries.
