About Us

ASS Kunya Nigeria Limited is a Company of Integrity, Quality and Timeliness

We are the Best Non-Oil Export Market

ASS Kunya Nigeria Limited is a Nigerian registered company with the Corporate Affairs Commission and was incorporated in September 2007. The company became a Limited Liability Company in December 2010. We are into the business of exporting of agricultural products.

We started our exporting business in 2011 and have since become one of the leading exporter of dried hibiscus flowers and bye-products as well as other agricultural products. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us one of the most trusted names in the industry. We are proud to offer our clients premium-quality products at competitive prices.
Now we export a wide range of goods and services to countries in Europe, America, Asia and the Caribbean. We are committed to creating value for our clients by providing quality products and services. You can learn more about us at faralresources with the button below.
